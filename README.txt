Spellathon
==========

Author
------

Instructions
------------
1. Launch Spellathon by running project1.sh or spellathon.py.
2. On your initial launch, click 'Administrate' on the logon screen to create the administrator account.
3. Once the account is created, enter the administrator account details and click 'Administrate' again in order to make word lists and manage users.
3. Create some lists in the list management window.
4. Go back to the logon screen by logging out.
5. You can play the lists you have created by logging in with your administrator account or making a new user account.
